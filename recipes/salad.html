Legendary Salad

A refreshing and quick salad for those hot and sunny days.

Ingredients

  • Chick peas
  • Green pepper
  • Black olives
  • A garlic clove
  • Mint leaves
  • Cilantro leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and mix it with the oil, lemon, salt and pepper
  2. Add the rinsed chick peas
  3. Slice the green pepper in strips
  4. Finely chop the mint and cilantro leaves
  5. Mix everthing with the rest of the Ingredients
  6. Add salt, pepper and oil to taste.
  7. Enjoy!
