Everything in the Fridge Wraps

Wondering what to do with all those ingredients left in recipients in the fridge? Just put them together in a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Rice from a week ago
  • The wrinled remaining of a pepper you used a week ago and left in the fridge
  • Black olives
  • Lettuce. Doesn't mind if it's turning yellow
  • Chicken, canned tuna, whatever protein you have
  • Corn
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop everything
  2. Season the chicken or meat with salt and pepper and chop in medium sized cubes
  3. Cook at high heat
  4. Put the tortillas in a wide pan at low heat so they heat but don't get toasted
  5. Put the cheese on top of the tortillas. Let them on the pan until the cheese melts
  6. Put everything on top of the tortilla
  7. Add salt and pepper on vegetables
  8. Enjoy!
